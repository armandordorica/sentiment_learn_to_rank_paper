CRSP crsp.msedelist records why a stock left the market (dlstcd) and the return on exit (dlret). Cached for all 1,000 universe PERMNOs — only missing names are queried from WRDS.

2E · 💵 Cash-merger exits

For stocks that left the market via a cash merger (CRSP dlstcd 232/233), the final-week return is recovered from CRSP dlret, estimated from the SDC M&A deal price, or set to 0 as a last-price fallback. Only missing PERMNOs are queried from WRDS.
